Welcome
to The Anchor Theatre company

The Anchor was founded in 2015 by Katie Cawthorne, Laura Lethlean and Jessica Arthur.
Through exploring the truth that comes with human emotion, the three set out to tackle subjects that are frequently judged but rarely discussed to make theatre that reconsiders forgotten or excluded stories.

The Anchor Theatre Company lends heart to the future of theatre that looks to the future and is not manacled by tradition but has learnt from it.

- Peter Wilkins, Canberra Critics Circle, November 2015.

The development of The Anchor as a theatre company is something to which theatre goers should pay attention.

- Tim Garrett, Theatre People, October 2018.

Since 2015, the Anchor has produced work in Canberra, Sydney and Melbourne on subjects including abortion, gender expectations, motherhood, legacy and drought. The Anchor uses theatre an equaliser by allowing all voices concerned to be valued and heard. The intent of each production is to increase compassion and connection within the audience.

The Anchor Theatre Company has provided new and insightful theatrical challenges that are dramatically absorbing and daring in style and technique.

- Flora Georgiou, Stage Whispers, November 2018.

I look forward to what the Anchor Theatre Company produces next.

- Irene Bell, Theatre Press, May 2019

The Anchor places an emphasis on visual and physical theatre, which results in slick and technically impressive productions.
