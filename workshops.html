Workshops

The Anchor believes in the power of storytelling through self-representation. Therefore, the company runs workshops that allow spaces for people to tell their own stories.

These workshops culminate in theatrical performances that engage audiences and communities with the direct experiences of the performers. The workshops develop the confidence and performance skills of the individuals, enabling a strong sense of ensemble and shared ownership of the outcome.

Please contacts us at theanchorth@gmail.com for more information, including workshop outlines.
